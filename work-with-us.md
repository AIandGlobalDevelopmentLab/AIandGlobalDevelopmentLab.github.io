## For Prospective PhD Students

We accept PhD students through the graduate programs at our respective institutions:

**UT Austin** — Department of Government
Apply through the [Government PhD program](https://liberalarts.utexas.edu/government/graduate/). Mention interest in the AI & Global Development Lab in your application.

**Linköping University** — Institute for Analytical Sociology
Apply through the [IAS doctoral program](https://liu.se/en/organisation/liu/iei/ias).

**Harvard** — Department of Statistics
Apply through the [Statistics PhD program](https://statistics.fas.harvard.edu/graduate).

## For Postdoctoral Researchers

We occasionally have postdoctoral positions funded through grants. If you have your own funding or are applying for fellowships, we're happy to discuss hosting arrangements.

Ideal candidates have:
- Strong methodological training (statistics, machine learning, econometrics)
- Interest in global development applications
- Experience with satellite imagery, survey data, or both
- Commitment to open science and reproducible research

---

## For Research Assistants

We hire undergraduate and graduate research assistants on a project basis. RA positions typically involve:
- Data processing and cleaning
- Literature reviews
- Software development and testing
- Visualization and mapping

Email us with your CV and a brief description of your interests.

---

## Collaboration

We welcome collaborations with researchers at other institutions. If you're working on related problems and see potential for joint work, reach out.

We're particularly interested in collaborations that:
- Extend our methods to new domains
- Provide access to novel data sources
- Bridge research and policy
